## What's New in v1.2.0

We're thrilled to release the biggest Road Hopper update yet! Here's everything that's new:

### Daily Challenge Mode

Every day brings a new challenge with unique objectives and rewards. Complete daily challenges to earn exclusive badges and bonus coins.

### Night-Time Road Theme

A beautiful new night-time road theme with neon-lit highways and glowing obstacles. Unlock it by completing 7 daily challenges.

### Performance Improvements

- Reduced memory usage by 20%
- Improved frame stability on mid-range devices
- Faster app startup time

### Bug Fixes

- Fixed collision detection edge cases
- Resolved leaderboard sync delays
- Fixed UI overlap on small screens
