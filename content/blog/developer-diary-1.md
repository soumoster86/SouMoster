## The Beginning

Every developer has an origin story. Mine started with a simple question: *Why aren't there more fun, polished indie games on Android?*

I grew up playing mobile games during commutes, lunch breaks, and lazy Sundays. But too often, the games that caught my eye were either loaded with aggressive ads or lacked the polish I craved.

## The Vision

SouMoster was born from a desire to change that. I wanted to build games that:

1. **Respect the player** — No intrusive ads, fair monetization
2. **Feel premium** — Smooth animations, clean UI, satisfying feedback
3. **Are genuinely fun** — Easy to learn, hard to master

## Road Hopper

Road Hopper was our first project. The concept was simple: one-tap lane hopping on an endless road. But simplicity is deceptive — getting the feel right took dozens of iterations.

We tuned the hop timing, obstacle spacing, and visual feedback until every tap felt satisfying. That attention to detail is what SouMoster is all about.

## What's Next

We're already prototyping new game ideas. Each one will follow the same philosophy: fun first, polish always.

Stay tuned — the best is yet to come.
